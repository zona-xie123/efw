  2. Types of personal data : name, date of birth, national ID number or passport number, contact information (including but not limited to telephone number, e-mail or address) or other information that can directly or indirectly identify you personally.
  3. Pursuant to Article 3 of the Personal Data Protection Law,you may exercise the following rights over your own information held by the Bank: (1)Except for the circumstances described in Article 10 of PDPA, the right to inquire, review or obtain duplicate copies of information held, for which the Bank is permitted under Article 14 of PDPA to collect a small fee to cover its costs. (2) The right to supplement or rectify personal data held by the Bank. However, Article 19 of the Implementation Rules of the Personal Data Protection Act requires you to state reasons and facts. (3) The right to stop the Bank from collecting personal data according to Article 11, Paragraph 4 of PDPA in the event that the Bank is found to have violated PDPA while collecting, processing or using your personal data. (4) The right to stop the Bank from processing, using, or limited processing of your personal data according to Article 11, Paragraph 2 of PDPA, should you have doubts toward the correctness of information held by the Bank. This excludes situations where you have consented in writing or situations where the Bank is required to continue its use of the information as part of its business activities, provided that the area of dispute has been remarked clearly. (5) The right to stop the Bank from processing, using, or limited processing of your personal data, and to have personal data deleted from the Bank's database according to Article 11, Paragraph 3 of PDPA, when the purpose for which information was collected no longer exists or when the duration expires. This excludes situations where you have consented in writing or situations where the Bank is required to continue its use of the information as part of its business activities. (6)The right to have the Bank take out or transfer your personal data. (applies only to data subjects within the EU) (7)The right to have the Bank withdraw your consent to the personal data declaration. (applies only to data subjects within the EU) (8)The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe your data protection rights have been infringed by the Bank.
  4. You are free to choose whether to provide your personal data to E.SUN Bank. However, if the personal data provided by you is reported or if E.SUN Bank finds that it is not sufficient to confirm the authenticity of your identity or there exists any circumstances of fraudulent use or misuse of personal data, or you refuse to provide your personal data, E.SUN Bank reserves the right to disqualify you from the contest. We seek your understanding in this matter.
